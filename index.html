About Us

Noor International is a reliable textile sourcing and manufacturing partner, serving global markets with high-quality home, hotel, hospitality, healthcare, and workwear textiles.

We specialize in woven made-ups, including bedsheets, pillowcases, towels, blankets, duvets, and workwear. Our operations are supported by an in-house manufacturing facility with over 100 looms and an annual production capacity exceeding 4.6 million units. Every product is developed with a strong focus on durability, consistency, and international quality standards. Through careful yarn selection, precise fabric construction, and strict quality control, we ensure each order meets the exact specifications agreed with our customers.

Beyond manufacturing, we help buyers overcome sourcing challenges through dependable timelines, transparent communication, and consistent quality. At Noor Internationals, we build long-term partnerships based on trust, performance, and reliability.
